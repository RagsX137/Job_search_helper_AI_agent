 About the role

We are looking for a Senior Machine Learning Scientist to join our team and help us build out our long term AI strategy. You will be integral in creating innovative machine learning models and refining existing ones to drive the future of our AI initiatives. You will be working closely with a team of ML scientists and engineers to design and implement cutting-edge solutions that address our unique business challenges. You'll have the opportunity to work on complex problems in a highly innovative environment, collaborate with cross-functional teams and contribute to the design, development, and deployment of robust and scalable AI solutions.. Your work will directly impact the growth and vision of Coalition, as you help us transform raw data into unique ML applications, pushing the boundaries of AI technology.

Responsibilities


    Drive and execute AI projects/products end-to-end: from ideation, analysis, prototyping, development, metrics, and monitoring.

    Design and implement ML pipelines for data preprocessing, feature engineering, model training, hyperparameter tuning, and model evaluation, enabling rapid experimentation and iteration.

    Survey and incorporate recent advances in AI/ML research into our set of core capabilities to drive the future of decisioning at Coalition

    Thoughtfully communicate your work and experience in applied machine learning through internal collaborations and mentorship opportunities.

    Contribute and grow the culture of data excellence on our team by bringing mature empathy, best practices, and processes to our organization.


Skills and Qualifications


    Ph.D. or MS degree in a quantitative or computational field 

    7+ years of proven track record of technical full-time work experience leading work to develop and deploy machine learning and data-based solutions in production

    Proficiency with NLP, particularly broad experience delivering end-to-end, high-quality LLM and RAG solutions with measurable impact

    Practical and extensive experience in supervised and unsupervised machine learning methods

    Expertise in statistical analysis methods, most notably regression analysis, statistical inference, and forecasting

    Proficiency in Python and object-oriented programming

    Effective in ambiguous problem spaces; ability to own and drive projects with minimal oversight and process 

    Exceptional written and oral communication with technical and non-technical audiences

    Comfortable in fast-paced startup environment
